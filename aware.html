Aware — Module 01

What Is a
Digital Footprint?

Every action you take online leaves data behind. Your digital footprint is the trail of information you create, share, and leave exposed — whether you know it or not.

The Three Types

Understanding your
digital trail

Active
What You Choose to Share
Social media posts, online forms, and anything you deliberately publish. You control this — but most people share far more than they intend to.
Passive
Collected Without You Knowing
Browsing history, location data, and behavioral data collected silently in the background. You did not choose to share this. It was taken.
Persistent
Stays Even After Deletion
Cached pages, data broker records, and information already sold to third parties. Deletion does not equal erasure.
Your Digital Footprint

Your data trails
follow you everywhere.

Every website you visit, every search you make, and every app you open builds your digital footprint. Location data, browsing history, purchasing behavior, and social activity are collected, stored, and sold — most of the time without your knowledge.

01

Location Services

Your movements tracked each time an app accesses GPS — many continue in the background.

02

Apps and Platforms

80+ apps on the average smartphone. Most request access far beyond what they need.

03

Data Brokers

Third-party trackers compile and sell your personal data legally, without your consent.

Take Control

Security starts with
awareness.

Understanding what is collected, where it goes, and who buys it is the foundation of digital safety. Your footprint stays online longer than you think — but awareness is the first step toward reclaiming control.
