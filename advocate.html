Why Representation Matters

Cybersecurity needs
more voices.

Cybersecurity shapes national security policy, privacy law, corporate liability, and the legal rights of every person online. Women make up approximately 25% of the cybersecurity workforce. That gap means missing perspectives in every room where decisions are made.

Cipher and Counsel exists to help close it — building pathways, providing resources, and creating a community for the next generation of women in cyber, policy, and law.

The Problem
A Representation Gap With Real Consequences
When security teams lack diverse perspectives, the threats they miss and the policies they write reflect that absence.
The Opportunity
Interdisciplinary Leadership Is in Demand
Practitioners who bridge technical security, legal accountability, and policy communication are among the most valuable professionals in the field.
