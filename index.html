Why It Matters

Your data is worth
more than you think.

01
Personal Privacy
Your location history, health searches, financial behavior, and personal relationships are all part of your digital profile — and available to those who pay for access. Most people have no idea how much is already out there.
02
Account Security
Information in your digital footprint — your birthday, employer, hometown — is often the answer to security questions protecting your most important accounts. Oversharing publicly hands attackers the keys.
03
Legal Exposure
Data collected about you can be used in legal proceedings, background checks, immigration decisions, and insurance assessments — often without your knowledge or consent. This is a legal problem, not just a technical one.
04
National Security
State-sponsored actors actively target civilian digital infrastructure. Your personal data, aggregated at scale, contributes to intelligence profiles used by foreign adversaries. Individual habits have national consequences.
