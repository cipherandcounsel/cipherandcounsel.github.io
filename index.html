Our Why

Why Cipher and Counsel

Cipher and Counsel was built to make cybersecurity understandable - and to create space for women to lead in cyber, policy, and law.

As technology continues to shape our daily lives, understanding how to protect your digital footprint is no longer optional. It is essential. Most people are unaware of how much of their personal data is collected, shared, and stored - and even fewer know their legal rights around it.

This platform exists to change that. Through education, practical guidance, and advocacy, Cipher and Counsel makes cybersecurity accessible to everyone while building the next generation of women leaders in cybersecurity, policy, and digital law.
