Why It Matters

Your data is worth
more than you think.

Your digital footprint is used by advertisers to target you, employers to evaluate you, insurers to assess you, and bad actors to exploit you.

01
Personal Privacy
Your location, health searches, and financial behavior are part of a profile available to those who pay.
02
Legal Exposure
Your data can appear in legal proceedings, background checks, and insurance decisions without your knowledge.
03
National Security
State-sponsored actors target civilian infrastructure. Individual habits have consequences at a national scale.
